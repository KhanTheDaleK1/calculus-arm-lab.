Featured: Equations Synth

Equations Synth is a commercial, third-party virtual instrument from Decent Samples. It runs as a plugin inside a compatible audio host and renders its sound engine locally on your machine.

How it works: you load the instrument in your DAW or player, then it generates audio using its licensed engine and content in real time, just like other pro audio plugins.

Why it is not embedded here: the product EULA does not allow redistribution or web embedding of the instrument or its content, so we cannot bundle it directly into this site. Instead, we link to the official product page and share the demo video.
